## Call for Proposals

In May, three conferences have opened the CfP for AOSP and AAOS releated content:

### AOSP devroom at IndiaFOSS

> Call for Proposals: AOSP devroom at IndiaFOSS 2026!
>
> The Android Open Source Project (AOSP) devroom is returning to IndiaFOSS this
> September in Bengaluru! In collaboration with the aosp-devs community, we are
> inviting developers and tinkerers to submit their talk proposals. From deep
> dives into AOSP and Android Automotive OS framework to custom ROMs and AI
> assisted development, if it's open-source Android, we want to hear about it!
>
> Whether you are a seasoned contributor or a passionate newcomer, this is your
> platform to share your Android insights and war stories with the community.
>
> Get all the details and submit your session (make sure to select the "AOSP"
> track): [https://fossunited.org/indiafoss/2026/devrooms/aosp](https://fossunited.org/indiafoss/2026/devrooms/aosp)
>
> CFP Deadline: June 15, 2026
>
> Event: Sept 26-27, 2026, NIMHANS Convention Centre, Bengaluru


### Android MC at LPC

> Call for Proposals: Android Micro Conference at the Linux Plumbers Conference!
>
> The Android Micro Conference at LPC brings the upstream Linux community and Android systems developers together to discuss platform challenges, kernel interactions and dependencies, and opportunities for upstream collaboration.
>
> If you are working on subsystems or features that bridge Android and the upstream Linux kernel, we want your voice in the room. Submit your discussion topics and abstracts here (please select the "Android MC" track): [https://lpc.events/event/20/abstracts/](https://lpc.events/event/20/abstracts/)
>
> Note: We encourage you to submit your proposals by June 26, 2026 to take advantage of the pre-registration window.
>
> CFP Deadline: August 07, 2026
>
> Event: October 05-07, 2026, Prague Congress Centre, Prague

## AOSP goes agentic

[lightrion.com](https://lightrion.com/) popped up on Discord and LinkedIn. It's an MCP server for AOSP:

> Source-grounded answers for Android code questions.

For a quickstart, go to [search.lightrion.com](https://search.lightrion.com/) and
see the [Discussion on Discord](https://discord.com/channels/1294292505419841678/1294292505927487510/1505113085071851630)
and [LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/building-aosp-code-search-service-ai-dev-tools-report-daniel-fages-dt5ve/).

There is a blogpost about using claude code on AOSP:
[Using Claude Code on AOSP-scale projects](https://utzcoz.github.io/2026/04/26/using-claude-code-on-aosp-scale-projects.html).
Further [discussion here on Discord](https://discord.com/channels/1294292505419841678/1294292505927487510/1503799763533828147)

And there is a new open book about Android on Github (to also feed the AI): [A Developer's Guide to the Android Open Source Project](https://aospbooks.github.io/aosp-internal-book/)
